# Text Elements
Arabic letters have 4 variations
 - The beginning of a word (B)
 - The middle of a word (M)
 - The end of a word (E)
 - When the letter is separate (S) ^aBLJEmxI

For example: ^ywVUONa2

Group 1 ^ie7P5ZeM
